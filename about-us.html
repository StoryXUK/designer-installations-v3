About Designer Installations

Welcome to Designer Installations, where luxury meets craftsmanship in every bathroom we create. With a passion for elevating your home experience, we are the premier choice for discerning homeowners across the South of England.

Our Philosophy

Luxury goes beyond aesthetics; it's about the integration of premium materials, cutting-edge technology, and personalised touches. Designer Installations takes pride in redefining luxury bathrooms.

Luxury Redesigned

We believe that your bathroom should be a sanctuary—a space where luxury and functionality seamlessly converge. Our vision is to transform your vision into reality, crafting bespoke bathrooms that reflect your unique style and enhance the beauty of your home.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in the meticulous craftsmanship that goes into each installation. From contemporary spa-inspired retreats or a classic, timeless design, our team will bring your vision to life.
